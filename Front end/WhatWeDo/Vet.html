PROVIDING MEDICAL NEED FOR INJURED ANIMALS

A fully-equipped, premier medical facility for pets, We offer inpatient care with separate wards for cats and dogs. We offer; spacious surgical suites, the latest diagnostic imaging equipment, a dedicated imaging department including radiography and ultrasound, on-site laboratory with on-the-spot tests, dental services for inpatients and trained veterinary technicians - so that your pet receives the best care possible. We focus on offering an array of medical and health services and care on par with the best in the world.
