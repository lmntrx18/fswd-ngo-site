Why Donate?

We Save Animals focus on animal welfare work to improve the lives of animals that may be abandoned, mistreated, or neglected. We also work to combat animal cruelty, which can take many forms and to promote responsible pet ownership

By donating, you can help provide these animals with the care they need, such as shelter, food, and medical treatment and help them investigate and prosecute animal cruelty cases, and work to prevent animal abuse from happening in the first place.You can also help promote responsible pet ownership in your community.
