FORGING NEW LIVES FOR INDIA’S FORGOTTEN ANIMALS

India’s stray animal population numbers nearly 90 million. However, India’s shelter system already faces population stress with less than 9 million animals. We Save Animal’s work finding forever homes for these neglected animals not only gives each pet the love they deserve but frees up critically important housing and veterinary capacity for those remaining in shelters.

BANGALORE, INDIA ADOPTIONS

If you live within 100km of Hyderabad, India, we’d love to receive your application to adopt a former Indian street dog. Our local staff is standing by to help you change an abandoned dog’s life forever.

US ADOPTIONS

For people seeking pets in the US, our network can bring your new family member right to your door! While this is a lengthy and costly process, you will completely change your new best friend’s life, forever saving an animal from a life on the streets.

UK ADOPTIONS

We bring dogs in India to your home in the UK! Please note: this is not a short or cheap process, but you will completely change the life of the Indian dog you bring to their home, saving it from a life on the streets.
