Beyond cash and your budget, your time, and your distinctive skills could all be valuable and impactful. If you’d like to join the fight for India’s forgotten animals, we’d love to have you. Check out our volunteer opportunities below.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES IN BANGALORE, INDIA

If you’re near our Bangalore shelter, stop in any time from 10 a.m.–4 p.m., no appointment is necessary. We’re in need of Adoption Managers, Photographers, and Social Media Managers to help our animals find loving homes.
